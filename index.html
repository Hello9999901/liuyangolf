Yan Liu, LPGA Teaching Professional, Class A

As a member of LPGA Teaching Professionals for over twenty years, Yan has devoted much of her time to the game of golf. Recently Yan was named LPGA’s Global Evaluator who will educate and test new members. She was also appointed LPGA’s Golf Ambassador for China. Over the years she has taught hundreds of students , including beginners and skilled players, men and women, adults and juniors. She is able to help her students improve their games and reach their full potentials.
